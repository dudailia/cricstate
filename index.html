How much predictive signal is there in cricket beyond match state?

A calibrated, leakage-audited benchmark on 16,754 matches asks whether the two features practitioners reach for next — player identity and match-level conditions — add anything over a strong state model. Measured under a decision rule fixed before the test split was ever touched.

Match state is saturating. A gradient-boosted model of within-match state captures almost all of the recoverable-above-marginal structure. The two obvious enrichments are the near-invisible slivers on the right — and neither clears the bar.
